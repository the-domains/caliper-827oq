Who is Maria Mitchell?

Women's Stories / YouTube
Published on March 1, 2014. Who is Maria Mitchell? Maria Mitchell was a famous astronomer and educator in the 19th century United States. She inspired many young women to pursue scientific and educational careers. This is the inaugural video of Women's Stories, a YouTube channel dedicated to publishing stories of women to inspire girls, encourage young women and celebrate women's lives.
